This Lost and Found Portal is for Amrita School Of Engineering, Bengaluru. With the help of this students and teachers can find what they have left behind or turn in something they found on campus. Found Listings consists of all items found that have not been claimed. After end of each semester,all items not claimed will be discarded.

How It Works

Check in the Found Listings page if your missing item is there. If it is please go to administration office to pick it up. Also please bring a valid proof to claim the item.

In case missing item is not in Found Listing login or sign up. Then fill missing item form which can be viewed only by the administrator. If missing item is found the administrator will notify the candidate. If the candidate themselves have found the item, please inform administrator.

To add an item in Found Listing page, inform administrator. The administrator will add the item to the list.
